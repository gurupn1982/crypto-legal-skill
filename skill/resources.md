## Official Regulatory Resources

### United States

- [SEC Digital Asset Framework](https://www.sec.gov/corpfin/framework-investment-contract-analysis-digital-assets) — Howey test analysis
- [SEC FinHub](https://www.sec.gov/finhub) — Digital assets regulatory guidance
- [FinCEN Virtual Currency Guidance](https://www.fincen.gov/resources/statutes-regulations/guidance/application-fincens-regulations-persons-administering) — MSB rules
- [OFAC SDN List](https://ofac.treasury.gov/sanctions-programs-and-country-information) — Sanctions screening
- [IRS Virtual Currency FAQ](https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/virtual-currencies)
- [CFTC Digital Assets](https://www.cftc.gov/digitalassets/index.htm) — Derivatives / commodities

### European Union

- [MiCA Full Text (EUR-Lex)](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX%3A32023R1114)
- [ESMA MiCA Guidelines](https://www.esma.europa.eu/esmas-activities/digital-finance-and-innovation/markets-crypto-assets-regulation-mica)
- [EBA Crypto-Assets Monitoring](https://www.eba.europa.eu/regulation-and-policy/innovation-and-fintech/crypto-assets)

### Singapore

- [MAS Digital Payment Token (DPT) Services](https://www.mas.gov.sg/regulation/payments/digital-payment-token-services)
- [MAS Stablecoin Regulatory Framework](https://www.mas.gov.sg/regulation/payments/stablecoins)
- [IRAS Crypto Tax Guide](https://www.iras.gov.sg/taxes/individual-income-tax/basics-of-individual-income-tax/tax-residency-and-tax-rates/individual-income-tax-rates)

### UAE

- [VARA Virtual Assets Framework](https://www.vara.ae/en/)
- [ADGM FSRA Crypto Framework](https://www.adgm.com/fsra)
- [UAE MOF on Corporate Tax](https://mof.gov.ae/corporate-tax/)

### India

- [FIU-IND Registration Portal](https://fiuindia.gov.in/)
- [Income Tax India — VDA Provisions](https://www.incometaxindia.gov.in/)
- [SEBI Annual Reports](https://www.sebi.gov.in/)
- [RBI Master Directions on FEMA](https://www.rbi.org.in/scripts/NotificationUser.aspx)
